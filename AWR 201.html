Policies — attendance, late work, AI

Attendance: 2 absences allowed (excused + unexcused). Each additional absence significantly impacts the A/P grade. 5+ min late, 3+ times = marked "late."

Late work — Process Work: Accepted up to 24 hrs late. Penalty: −1 pt if worth < 10 pts; −2 pts if worth 10+ pts. Not accepted after 24 hrs.

Late work — Major Assignments: −10% per day. Not accepted after 48 hrs.

AI policy: AI use is prohibited in all stages (brainstorming, outlining, drafting, revision) unless the instructor gives express written permission. Violations may result in a zero, Academic Integrity charge, or course failure.
